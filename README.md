# 👕 Clothing Product Pricing Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the factors that influence clothing product prices using Microsoft Excel. The study examines how product attributes such as brand, category, material, size, and color affect pricing patterns and provides insights through data visualization and dashboard reporting.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze price variations across clothing brands.
- Examine the relationship between product categories and prices.
- Determine the impact of material types on pricing.
- Identify the highest-priced combinations of brand and category.
- Present findings through interactive dashboards and visualizations.

## 📊 Dataset Description
The dataset consists of the following variables:

- **Brand** – Clothing brand name
- **Category** – Type of clothing product
- **Color** – Product color
- **Size** – Clothing size
- **Material** – Fabric or material used
- **Price** – Selling price of the clothing product

## ❓ Research Questions
1. How does brand affect clothing prices?
2. Does product category significantly impact pricing?
3. What relationship exists between material type and price?
4. Do clothing sizes influence pricing?
5. How does color affect clothing prices?
6. Which combination of brand and category results in the highest-priced products?

## 🔍 Key Findings
- Product prices differ across brands and categories.
- Material type significantly influences clothing prices.
- **New Balance** has the highest average price among all brands.
- **Dresses** are the highest-priced product category on average.
- **White-colored** clothing items have the highest average selling price.
- Price differences across clothing sizes are relatively small.
- Dashboards and visualizations provide valuable insights for decision-making.
